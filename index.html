IWNLP: Inverse Wiktionary for Natural Language Processing

This page will be updated soon.

IWNLP consists of two parts:

  • IWNLP: A parser for the German edition of Wiktionary
  • :IWNLP.LemmatizerA German lemmatizer that uses the output from IWNLP to produce a mapping from an inflected form to a lemma.
